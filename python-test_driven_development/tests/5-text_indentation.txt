===========================
Tests for text_indentation module
===========================

Import the function:
    >>> text_indentation = __import__('5-text_indentation').text_indentation

--- Normal cases ---

Basic sentence with period:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello. World")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    World

Question mark:
    >>> text_indentation("How are you? Good")
    How are you?
    <BLANKLINE>
    Good

Colon:
    >>> text_indentation("Note: something")
    Note:
    <BLANKLINE>
    something

Multiple special characters:
    >>> text_indentation("Hi. Bye? Ok: Done")
    Hi.
    <BLANKLINE>
    Bye?
    <BLANKLINE>
    Ok:
    <BLANKLINE>
    Done

No special characters:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello World")
    Hello World

Empty string:
    >>> text_indentation("")

Single special character:
    >>> text_indentation(".")
    .
    <BLANKLINE>

No spaces at beginning of new line (spaces after delimiter are skipped):
    >>> text_indentation("Hello.   World")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    World

Text ending without special character:
    >>> text_indentation("Iam ruinas videres")
    Iam ruinas videres

Text with consecutive special characters:
    >>> text_indentation("Hi.?.:")
    Hi.
    <BLANKLINE>
    ?
    <BLANKLINE>
    .
    <BLANKLINE>
    :
    <BLANKLINE>
